# Weather Station v3.6.0 - The Connectivity & BLE Update

## 🚀 Key Features
*   **BLE Support (GATT)**: Added full support for Bluetooth Low Energy weather stations using a new `BleConnection` implementation.
*   **Composite Connection Router**: Implemented a unified `CompositeConnection` that automatically routes requests to Classic Bluetooth, BLE, or Simulator based on device hardware type.
*   **Visual BLE Branding**: Added a "BLE" badge to the device list to help users identify Low Energy hardware at a glance.

## 🛠️ Bug Fixes & Refinements
*   **Discovery Fix (Permissions)**: Resolved an issue where unpaired devices wouldn't appear on Android 12+ by correctly requesting `ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION` when `neverForLocation` is not specified.
*   **LiveData Reliability**: Fixed a race condition/update issue in the discovery list by ensuring new list instances are posted to LiveData.
*   **Android 14+ Readiness**: Updated BroadcastReceiver registrations with `RECEIVER_NOT_EXPORTED` flags for better security and compatibility.
*   **Visual Scan Progress**: Added a progress spinner and "Searching..." status bar at the bottom of the device list fragment.
*   **Enhanced Logging**: Added detailed Timber logging for the entire discovery and connection lifecycle (Classic and BLE).

## 🧪 Quality & Tests
*   **New Test Suite**: Added `BluetoothDiscoveryTest.java` (Robolectric) to verify discovery states and broadcast handling.
*   **Build Integrity**: Verified through the mandatory sequence: `./gradlew spotlessApply test build`.
